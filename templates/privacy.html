Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Spendly collects, uses, and protects information you provide when using our personal expense tracking service. We believe your financial data is yours, and we are committed to handling it with care and transparency.

1. Data We Collect

When you create a Spendly account, we collect the information you provide directly: your name, email address, and a password. When you use the service, we store the expense records you choose to add — including amount, category, date, and any description you write. We also collect basic technical information such as your IP address and browser type to keep your account secure and to diagnose service issues.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data you provide exclusively to operate Spendly for you: displaying your expenses, generating summaries and charts, and syncing your records across the devices you sign in from. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to advertisers or other third parties. We may use aggregated, anonymized statistics (for example, the total number of expenses logged across all users) to understand how the service is used and to improve it.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers and transmitted using industry-standard encryption (HTTPS). Access to your account is protected by your password, and we recommend choosing a strong, unique password and not sharing it. While we work hard to protect your information, no system is perfectly secure — please reach out to us immediately if you suspect any unauthorized access to your account.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly relies on a small number of trusted third parties to operate — for example, a cloud hosting provider for our servers and a transactional email service to send you account-related messages like password resets. These providers only receive the minimum information needed to perform their function and are contractually obligated to keep it confidential. We do not embed advertising trackers or share your data with marketing partners.

5. Your Choices and Rights

You can review and update your account information at any time from your profile settings. You can export a copy of your expense data or permanently delete your account and all associated records. If you have questions about your data or wish to exercise any of these options, please contact us using the details below.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how Spendly handles your data, please reach out to us at support@spendly.app.
